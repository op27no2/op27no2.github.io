What it does

Radar, the way it actually works

Live, everywhere, fast

Every US NEXRAD site, streaming the latest volume in about a second — no waiting on a tile server to catch up.

Decades of archive

Replay any storm from the public Level II archive, frame for frame — reproducible exactly, from tornado outbreaks to landfalling hurricanes.

2D · 3D · Volumetric

Flatten it to a classic sweep, tilt through the cone, or render the whole storm as a volume you can orbit.

Storm science built in

Model soundings and hodographs, velocity dealiasing and storm-relative motion, and a live GOES lightning overlay synced to the loop.

The great storms, curated

Landfalling hurricanes, violent tornadoes, and derechos — each with the radar loop pinned to the right site and moment. One tap to the radar.

Share the moment

Send a link to an exact radar moment. Anyone who taps it lands on the same loop, same frame — in the app, or here in the browser.
